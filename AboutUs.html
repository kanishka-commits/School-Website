ABOUT US

Welcome to City Montessori School (CMS), a beacon of education and peace, nestled in the heart of Bengaluru, India.

Our Vision:

Since our founding in 1959 by Dr. Jagdish Gandhi and Dr. Bharti Gandhi, our vision has remained steadfast: to nurture enlightened individuals who will contribute to a more just and peaceful world.

Our Philosophy:

At CMS, we are guided by the principles of universal values, global understanding, and service to humanity.

Our Campuses:

With numerous campuses strategically located across Lucknow, we are committed to making quality education accessible to a wide range of students.

Join Our Family:

We invite you to join the CMS family and experience the transformative power of education.
